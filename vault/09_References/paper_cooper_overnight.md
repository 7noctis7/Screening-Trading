# Cooper, Cliff & Gulen (2008) — Return Differences between Trading and Non-Trading Hours
**Citation.** Cooper, M. J., Cliff, M. T., & Gulen, H. (2008). *Return Differences between Trading and Non-Trading Hours: Like Night and Day.* SSRN 1004081.
**Résultat clé.** L'essentiel de la prime actions US se réalise **overnight** (close→open) ; les heures de séance sont ~plates.
